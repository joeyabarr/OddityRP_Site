Oddity Roleplay
is coming soon™

OddityRP aims to be the largest roleplaying community on S&Box by Facepunch. S&Box for all intents and purposes is Garry's Mod 2, however, being built on Source 2 has greatly increased the potential for a roleplaying server. SantosRP set the bar high, we want to raise it. With a team comprised of long time members of the Santos community, we hope this to seize this opportunity to fill the void SantosRP left behind.

Stay tuned and we'll let you know when we launch!

If you would like to receive news and sneak previews join the Discord!
